GG

“GOOD GAME” is a website created by school student, Hannah, as a response to the discrimination and lack of representation both in and behind the computer screen. Too many young gamers are abused and ostracized online on the grounds of race, religion, gender, skill level, age, sexuality and identity. GOOD GAME seeks to create a safe space for players from all walks of life to connect and engage in the games they love without fear of hate. The GOOD GAME movement also seeks to expand past the confines of www.GGgoodgame.com and encourage equality, sportsmanship and kindness around the gaming world.
